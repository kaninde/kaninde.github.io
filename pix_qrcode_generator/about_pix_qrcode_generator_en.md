# About Pix QR Code Generator

Welcome to **Pix QR Code Generator**, the ultimate app for generating and managing PIX QR Codes, designed to simplify your payments. With a simple and functional interface, Pix QR Code Generator helps you create PIX QR Codes from keys or Copy and Paste codes, organize your contacts, and track your transaction history.

---

## Features

### Free

- **PIX QR Code Generation:** Create QR Codes with one tap from your clipboard or by typing manually. Full validation of EMV (Copy and Paste) codes.
- **PIX History:** View previously generated PIX QR Codes, with item limits on the free plan.
- **Contacts and PIX Keys:** Organize contacts and associate PIX keys to generate codes more easily.
- **My Accounts:** Register bank accounts and institutions for quick access to your PIX keys.
- **Basic Statistics:** Track summaries of your PIX usage.
- **Offline Support:** Works completely without an internet connection.
- **Lightweight and Fast Design:** Minimalist interface focused on simplicity and usability.

### Premium (Subscription)

- **Advanced Statistics:** Detailed analytics, intelligent insights, and temporal usage patterns.
- **Export/Import Data:** Back up and migrate your data between devices.
- **QR Code Templates:** Ready-made models to speed up PIX code creation.
- **Advanced Creation Fields:** Configurable expiration date, GPS-based city, and other advanced features.
- **Unlimited History:** Save and favorite as many items as you need.
- **Custom Themes:** Personalize the app’s appearance.

### Ads (AdSense)

Some features can be used **temporarily** by watching an ad. For example: saving a QR Code to history, favoriting a history item, or exporting a contact. After watching the ad, the action is unlocked at that moment without requiring a premium subscription. Ads are optional and do not affect fully free features.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Does Pix QR Code Generator require internet access to work?**  
No. The app works completely offline. Internet access is only used for optional features such as ads or premium services.

**Do I need to create an account to use Pix QR Code Generator?**  
No. The app does not require account creation. Your data is stored locally on your device.

**Will I lose my data if I uninstall the app?**  
Yes. Since data is stored locally, uninstalling the app will permanently delete your history, contacts, and settings.

**Is Pix QR Code Generator suitable for beginners?**  
Yes. The app is designed for all users, from those using PIX for the first time to frequent users.

**Does Pix QR Code Generator process payments?**  
No. The app only generates PIX QR Codes. The actual payment is made within your bank’s app, which scans the QR Code or Copy and Paste code generated.

**What is included in the premium plan?**  
The premium plan includes advanced features such as detailed statistics, intelligent insights, QR Code templates, and export/import functionality for backup or device migration.
