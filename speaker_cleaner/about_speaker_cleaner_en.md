# About Speaker Cleaner

**Speaker Cleaner** helps you run **guided audio sessions** to support routine maintenance of your device speakers. The experience includes a short diagnostic-style flow, clear on-screen progress, and cleaning modes suited to different situations (such as dust, moisture residue, or a full pass). The app is designed for **quick sessions**, **simple guidance**, and optional access to **advanced** profiles when you want more.

---

## Features

- **Guided cleaning flow:** Step-by-step audio session with visible progress and hints on screen.
- **Speaker check before the session:** Brief guided steps to prepare a safer, clearer session.
- **Modes for different needs:** Profiles for different cleaning scenarios, including advanced options when unlocked.
- **Comfort-first audio:** You stay in control of volume; loud playback can damage hearing—keep it comfortable.
- **Works offline for the core flow:** The main cleaning experience can run without the internet; **network access** may be used for **optional ads** (for example, rewarded video to try advanced access for a limited time) and for **in-app purchases** or **restore purchases** through your app store.

---

## Important notice

**Speaker Cleaner does not repair hardware.** It may help you optimize **perceived** sound through a structured audio routine. It is **not** a substitute for professional repair if your device is damaged.

---

## Our goal

We want to make speaker maintenance **simple, transparent, and easy to repeat**—with clear expectations, safe listening habits, and optional upgrades when you choose them.

## Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

**Does Speaker Cleaner require an account?**  
No account is required for the core cleaning experience described above.

**Does Speaker Cleaner fix broken speakers?**  
No. The app is **not** hardware repair software. If you suspect physical damage, contact a qualified technician.

**Can loud volume hurt my hearing?**  
Yes. Use a **comfortable** volume. The app reminds you that **loud playback may damage hearing**.

**Do I need the internet to clean?**  
The **core session** can work offline. **Internet** may be needed for **ads**, **in-app purchases**, or **restoring purchases**, depending on what you use.

**What is “advanced” access?**  
Advanced profiles may be available through a **short rewarded video** (time-limited access) or through **premium / lifetime** options offered in the app, subject to store availability and pricing.

**Will I lose my data if I uninstall the app?**  
Data stored **only on your device** may be removed when you uninstall. Purchases are tied to your **store account** according to platform rules (restore may be available).

**Does the app only contain speaker cleaning?**  
The app may include **other optional features**. This page focuses on **Speaker Cleaner**’s speaker maintenance experience.
