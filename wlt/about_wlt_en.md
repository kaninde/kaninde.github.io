# About WiseLend Tracker (WLT)

Welcome to **WiseLend Tracker (WLT)** — a personal loan management app for your phone: track money you lend and borrow, register payments, and stay on top of balances without spreadsheets.

WLT helps you manage **people** (borrowers and lenders), **loans** (amounts, interest, schedules, and history), a **dashboard** with balances and alerts, **payer ranking**, **PDF reports**, and **message templates** for collections. **Loan data**, **person profiles**, **payments**, and **settings** are stored **locally** on your device (SQLite database `wlt.db`). You can **export** and **import** a structured backup (ZIP with a manifest) from the Data screen.

---

## Highlights

- **Home hub:** Quick access to persons, loans, reports, dashboard, ranking, templates, Premium, and Data.
- **Persons:** Register contacts, view details, and start loans from a person profile.
- **Loans:** Create and track lent or borrowed amounts; simple or compound interest; pause, resume, or change rates; freeze or renegotiate; full timeline; register payments; schedule future payments; attach receipts where supported.
- **Dashboard:** Amounts to receive and to pay, net balance, active loans, top debtors, good payers, and items that need attention.
- **Ranking:** Compare payer behavior (best and worst payers) using in-app scoring.
- **Reports:** Generate PDF summaries for your records.
- **Templates:** Reusable collection messages with placeholders filled from loan data.
- **Data:** Local backup and restore; full export/import is a Premium feature (see in-app access rules).
- **Settings:** Language, theme (mode and accent where available), reminders, and app information.
- **Privacy-first core:** Your financial records stay on the device unless you export a file or use features that need the internet (see the Privacy Policy).

## FAQ

**Do I need an account?**  
No. You can use WLT for the core local workflow without creating an account.

**Do I need internet?**  
Most loan tracking works **offline**. Internet is used when you choose features that need it (for example **ads**, **in-app purchases** or **subscriptions**, loading optional **remote configuration** such as an “other apps” carousel, or opening external links).

**Will I lose my data if I uninstall?**  
Local data lives on your device. Uninstalling the app can **permanently remove** that data unless you have exported a backup ZIP.

**Is WLT a bank or lender?**  
No. WLT is a **personal record-keeping tool**. It does not move money, grant credit, or provide legal or tax advice. You remain responsible for agreements with the people you lend to or borrow from.
