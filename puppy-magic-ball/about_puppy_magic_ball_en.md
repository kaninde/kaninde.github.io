# About Puppy Magic Ball

Welcome to **Puppy Magic Ball**, a fun and lighthearted app designed to help you make simple decisions with a touch of randomness and humor.

Just ask a yes-or-no question, tap the screen, and let the Puppy Magic Ball decide for you. Whether you are playing with friends, killing time, or just curious, the app delivers quick and entertaining answers.

---

## Features

- **Yes or No Answers:** Get instant responses to simple questions.
- **Playful Experience:** Cute visuals and a friendly vibe.
- **One-Tap Interaction:** Extremely simple and intuitive to use.
- **Offline First:** Works entirely without an internet connection.
- **No Account Required:** Open the app and start playing.

---

## Purpose

Puppy Magic Ball was created purely for entertainment. It is not intended to provide professional, serious, or reliable advice. All answers are generated randomly and should be treated as a game.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General

**Does Puppy Magic Ball require an internet connection?**  
No. Puppy Magic Ball works fully offline. An internet connection is only used for optional ads or future features, if available.

**Can I use Puppy Magic Ball without creating an account?**  
Yes. Puppy Magic Ball does not require any account or sign-up. Just open the app and start playing.

**Are the answers really random?**  
Yes. All answers are generated randomly and purely for entertainment purposes.

**Can I rely on Puppy Magic Ball to make important decisions?**  
No. Puppy Magic Ball is designed only for fun and casual use. It should not be used for serious, legal, medical, or financial decisions.

**Is Puppy Magic Ball suitable for all ages?**  
The app is intended for casual entertainment and simple fun. Parental guidance is recommended for younger users.

### Secret Mode

**What is Secret Mode?**  
Secret Mode is a hidden feature that lets you control the answer instead of it being random. When active, tapping the left half of the screen produces a **No** answer, and tapping the right half produces a **Yes** answer.

**How do I activate or deactivate Secret Mode?**  
Tap the screen 5 times quickly (within 1.5 seconds). You will feel a vibration pattern confirming the change. The same gesture toggles it on and off. You can also toggle it from the Settings screen.

**How do I know if Secret Mode is on or off?**  
When you toggle Secret Mode, the app provides vibration feedback.  
- Short vibration + long vibration ‚Üí turned **on**  
- Long vibration + short vibration ‚Üí turned **off**  
There is no visual indicator on screen, keeping it discreet.

**Does Secret Mode stay active after closing the app?**  
Yes. Secret Mode state is saved and persists between sessions.

**Does Secret Mode work on all screens?**  
The activation gesture works on the theme/avatar selection screen and on the gameplay screen. The left/right answer control applies only on the gameplay screen.

### Avatars

**How many avatars are available?**  
Puppy Magic Ball includes 24 different animated avatars, each with its own personality and responses.
