# About TaPago

Welcome to **TaPago**, the ultimate fitness check-in and progress tracking app designed to keep your motivation high. With a clean and social-friendly interface, TaPago helps you log your workouts, share achievements, and celebrate every step of your fitness journey.

---

## Features

- **Quick Check-ins:** Mark your workouts with a single tap.
- **Progress Tracking:** Visualize your consistency and weekly performance.
- **Lightweight & Fast:** Minimalist design focused on simplicity and usability.
- **Offline Support:** Works seamlessly without internet connection.

---

## Our Mission

Our goal is to help you stay consistent, motivated, and proud of your progress. TaPago is more than just a tracker — it’s a way to celebrate your discipline and connect with others who share the same drive.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Does TaPago require an internet connection?**  
No. TaPago works fully offline. An internet connection is only needed for optional features like ads or future social sharing.

**Can I use TaPago without creating an account?**  
Yes. TaPago does not require account creation. Your progress is stored locally on your device.

**Will I lose my data if I uninstall the app?**  
Yes. Since data is stored locally, uninstalling the app will remove your saved check-ins and progress.

**Is TaPago suitable for beginners?**  
Absolutely. TaPago is designed to be simple and motivating for all fitness levels — from beginners to experienced athletes.

**Is TaPago a workout plan or coaching app?**  
No. TaPago does not provide workout plans or coaching. It focuses on check-ins, consistency, and motivation.
