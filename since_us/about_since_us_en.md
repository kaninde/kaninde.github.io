# About Since Us

Welcome to **Since Us** — a couples app to keep your story close.

Since Us helps you build a shared timeline of memories, celebrate milestones (bodas), and keep meaningful moments in one place.

---

## Highlights

- **Timeline & memories:** Add moments and look back at your journey.
- **Bodas & milestones:** Celebrate what you’ve built together.
- **Photos (optional):** Personalize your space with the photos you choose.
- **Simple & private:** Designed with privacy in mind.

---

## FAQ

**Do I need an account?**  
No. You can use Since Us without creating an account.

**Do I need internet?**  
Many features work offline. Some optional services may require internet.

**Will I lose my data if I uninstall?**  
If your data is stored locally, uninstalling the app can remove it from your device.
