# About Pocket Color Lighting Studio (PCLS)

Welcome to **Pocket Color Lighting Studio (PCLS)** — a mobile app to turn your screen into a colored light, explore colors by name or code, and see how hues shift under different color temperatures.

PCLS helps you use the **Light Panel** (full-screen light with brightness control), the **Kelvin Simulator** (preview a base color under warm-to-cool illuminants), **Presets** (ready-made lighting scenes for selfie, ambient, video, and more), and **Color catalogs** (search thousands of named colors by HEX, RGB, CMYK, or locale). **Recent colors**, **custom catalog entries**, and **your own presets** are stored **locally** on your device (SQLite) unless you use features that need the network (see the Privacy Policy).

---

## Highlights

- **Light Panel:** Pick a color, adjust brightness, and use the screen as a practical light source; optional catalog search inline.
- **Kelvin Simulator:** Compare how the same color looks across standard Kelvin steps; compact or detailed views; export a comparison image when available.
- **Presets:** Browse categories (selfie, ambient, creators, cinematic, utility, and custom); open a preset directly in the Light Panel; save your own presets.
- **Color catalogs:** Search bundled color-name catalogs (multilingual names and aliases); browse, inspect HEX/RGB/CMYK attributes, and jump to Light Panel or Kelvin.
- **Color input:** Enter HEX, RGB, or CMYK; use the color wheel picker; get catalog suggestions while you type.
- **Color history:** Quick access to colors you used recently.
- **Settings:** App language, theme, and links to legal information.
- **Privacy-first core:** Your lighting choices and local catalog data stay on the device unless you use online features (ads, store billing, optional catalog sync, or external links).

## FAQ

**Do I need an account?**  
No. You can use the core local workflow without creating an account.

**Do I need internet?**  
Most color picking, Light Panel, Kelvin, and preset use work **offline** after catalogs are installed. Internet is used when you choose features that need it (for example **ads**, **in-app purchases**, **optional remote catalog updates**, optional **translation** helpers, or opening external links).

**Will I lose my data if I uninstall?**  
Local data lives on your device. Uninstalling the app can **permanently remove** history, custom colors, and presets unless you have backed up content through export features when available.

**Is this a professional colorimeter or studio monitor calibration tool?**  
No. PCLS is a **creative utility** for phone/tablet screens. You remain responsible for how you use screen light (eye comfort, brightness in dark environments, and any production or health requirements).
