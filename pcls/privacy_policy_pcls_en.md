# Privacy Policy – Pocket Color Lighting Studio (PCLS)

Last updated: May 18, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how **Pocket Color Lighting Studio** (“PCLS”, “App”) handles information on your device and, when you use certain features, limited interactions over the internet.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how data is handled.

---

## 2. Data Collection and Storage

### 2.1 Local data storage

- The App is designed so that **core content** — including **color history**, **lighting presets** you save, **color catalog** entries (bundled and user-added), **catalog search indexes**, **app settings** (language, theme, preferences), and related records — is stored **locally** on your device (for example in a local SQLite database).
- Where available, you can **delete** user-created catalog colors or presets from within the App, clear history, or remove data by uninstalling the App.

### 2.2 Accounts

- The App **does not require** you to create an account for the main local workflow.

### 2.3 Data we do not operate as a color cloud backend

- PCLS is **not** a professional color-management or studio calibration service and **does not** run a central product cloud whose purpose is to store your full color library on **our** servers as part of normal day-to-day use.
- When you use **share**, **export** (for example Kelvin comparison images), or open a file, content is sent through **your** device’s operating system. That is under **your** control.

### 2.4 Network use (store, ads, optional remote content)

When you choose features that need connectivity, the App may connect to the internet, for example to:

- **In-app purchases / subscriptions:** handled by **Google Play** or **Apple App Store** under their terms and privacy policies.
- **Advertising (if enabled):** ad SDKs may process **device and usage signals** as described in their own policies.
- **Optional catalog sync or remote JSON:** to check for updated color-catalog packages or configuration hosted on **third-party** URLs included in the build; such requests go to whoever operates that host.
- **Optional translation helpers:** when enabled, short text (for example a color-name query) may be sent to a **third-party** translation endpoint to suggest matches in another language; see the provider’s policy for that service.
- Opening **documentation**, **support**, or **store** links also uses the network.

### 2.5 Permissions

Depending on the features and platform, the App may request access to capabilities such as:

- **Internet:** for **ads**, **billing**, optional catalog sync, optional translation, or opening links.
- **Keep screen on / wake lock (Light Panel):** so the display can stay lit while you use the panel.
- **Files / storage (as required by the OS):** for **exporting** or **sharing** images when those features are available.

The App does not access these resources without **your** action where the system requires explicit consent.

---

## 3. Data usage

Data stored or entered locally is used to:

- Run the **Light Panel**, **Kelvin Simulator**, **Presets**, **Color catalogs**, **color history**, and **settings** screens;
- Power **catalog search** (names, aliases, HEX/RGB/CMYK parsing);
- Apply **optional remote catalog updates** when you trigger sync;
- Show **ads** or **premium** flows when enabled in your build.

We do **not** use your color choices or custom catalog labels to build an advertising profile **on our side**; third-party ad networks may process data under **their** policies when ads are shown.

---

## 4. Data sharing

- We **do not sell** your personal information.
- Your **local color and preset content** is not uploaded to **our** servers as part of the default local workflow described above.
- **App stores**, **ad networks**, **translation providers**, and **hosts of optional remote URLs** you fetch may process data according to **their** policies when you use those services.

Any sharing of exported images or screenshots happens only when **you** initiate share, export, or copy.

---

## 5. Third-party services

The App may rely on third parties, including:

- **Advertising:** may collect identifiers or usage data for ad delivery and measurement.
- **Payments:** processed by the platform store.
- **Hosts of optional configuration or catalog files:** whoever operates the URL you fetch may see **technical request data** (such as IP address) like any web server.
- **Optional translation services:** may receive the text you submit for translation when that feature is used.

Those services operate under their own terms and privacy policies.

---

## 6. Data retention and deletion

- Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
- You can use in-app options to **clear history** or **remove user catalog entries or presets** where provided.
- Uninstalling the App typically **permanently removes** local databases from the device.

## 7. Security

We apply reasonable practices in the App, including keeping lighting-related content **on-device** by default and minimizing unnecessary collection.

You are responsible for securing your device (PIN, password, biometrics) and keeping the OS updated.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in a way that violates this intent.
