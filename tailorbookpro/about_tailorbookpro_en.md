# About TailorBook Pro

Welcome to **TailorBook Pro** — an app to run your tailoring atelier from your phone: clients, measurements, orders, and deliveries, with data stored on your device.

TailorBook Pro helps you register **clients** and **body profiles**, record **measurement sheets**, manage **service orders**, track **production**, **agenda**, and **finance**, and work with **pattern models** and **size tables**. Your atelier data stays **locally** on the device (SQLite). You can **export** and **import** a structured backup (ZIP with a manifest) from the Data area when you have premium access.

---

## Highlights

- **Home / dashboard:** shortcuts for new orders, clients, agenda, and finance.
- **Clients and profiles:** registration, contacts, notes, and body profiles per client.
- **Measurements:** measurement sheets, history, unit conversion, and session comparison (comparison is premium).
- **Orders:** drafts, line items, due dates, statuses, and links to measurements and production.
- **Agenda:** fittings, deliveries, and atelier appointments.
- **Production:** configurable stages (for example cut, sew, fitting, ready).
- **Finance:** payments, pending balances, and per-order records.
- **Pattern models and size tables:** reusable templates and P/M/G or numeric rules.
- **PDF:** service orders, measurement sheets, and receipts (premium).
- **Settings:** language, theme, units, currency, and atelier preferences.
- **Privacy-first core:** your client and order data stay on the device unless you export a file or use features that need the internet (see the Privacy Policy).

## Included features and limits (summary)

**Free tier (freemium):**

- Up to **3 clients** and **3 orders** total (**cumulative** count — deleting does not free slots).
- Agenda, production, finance, pattern models, size tables, and basic measurements without a daily usage cap.
- Banner ads in the main navigation.

**Premium** — unlock via **subscription**, **rewarded ad** (10 minutes of global premium), or **offline lifetime purchase**:

- Unlimited clients and orders.
- Generate PDFs and export/import full ZIP backups.
- Full themes (light/dark mode and extra color presets).
- No banner ads; “More apps” carousel hidden with premium.

Limits and entitlements can change with app updates; the **in-app** experience and store listing prevail over this page.

## FAQ

**Do I need an account?**  
No. You can use the core local workflow without creating an account.

**Do I need internet?**  
Day-to-day atelier work runs **offline**. Internet is used when you choose features that need it (for example **ads**, **in-app purchases**, loading optional **remote configuration** such as an “other apps” carousel, or opening external links).

**Will I lose my data if I uninstall?**  
Local data lives on your device. Uninstalling the app can **permanently remove** that data unless you have exported a backup ZIP (premium feature).

**Does it replace a cloud business system?**  
No. TailorBook Pro is a **local tool** for your atelier. You remain responsible for the accuracy of measurements, deadlines, and amounts you enter.
