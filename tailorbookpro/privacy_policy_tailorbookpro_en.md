# Privacy Policy – TailorBook Pro

Last updated: June 10, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how **TailorBook Pro** (“App”) handles information on your device and, when you use certain features, limited interactions over the internet.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how data is handled.

---

## 2. Data Collection and Storage

### 2.1 Local data storage

- The App is designed so that **core content** — including **clients** (for example name, contacts, and notes), **body profiles**, **measurement sheets and history**, **service orders** and line items, **agenda**, **production and finance records**, **pattern models**, **size tables**, **locally generated documents**, **settings**, and related records — is stored **locally** on your device (for example in a local SQLite database `tailorbookpro.db`).
- Where available with premium access, you can **export** a structured backup (typically a **ZIP** with a manifest), **import** a compatible backup, or **delete** data from within the App (including a full atelier wipe option in Settings).

### 2.2 Accounts

- The App **does not require** you to create an account for the main local workflow.

### 2.3 Data we do not operate as an atelier backend

- TailorBook Pro is **not** a cloud business-management service and **does not** run a backend whose purpose is to store your full client and order ledger on **our** servers as part of normal day-to-day use.
- When you use **share**, **export**, **generate PDF**, or open a file, content is sent through **your** device’s operating system (for example to another app or a path you choose). That is under **your** control.

### 2.4 Network use (store, ads, optional remote content)

When you choose features that need connectivity, the App may connect to the internet, for example to:

- **In-app purchases / subscriptions:** handled by **Google Play** or **Apple App Store** under their terms and privacy policies.
- **Advertising (if enabled):** ad SDKs may process **device and usage signals** as described in their own policies.
- **Optional remote JSON or links:** for example a lightweight **“other apps”** carousel or similar configuration hosted on a **third-party** URL included in the build; such requests go to whoever operates that host. Opening **documentation**, **support**, or **store** links also uses the network.

### 2.5 Permissions

Depending on the features and platform, the App may request access to capabilities such as:

- **Files / storage (as required by the OS):** for **importing** backup ZIPs, **exporting** backups, **saving PDFs**, and shares, using system pickers where available.
- **Internet:** for **ads**, **billing**, optional remote configuration, or opening links.
- **Camera or gallery (as applicable):** if you choose to attach photos to clients or documents, in line with the permissions declared for your build.

The App does not access these resources without **your** action where the system requires explicit consent.

---

## 3. Data usage

Data you provide is used to:

- Run **clients**, **measurements**, **orders**, **agenda**, **production**, **finance**, **pattern models**, and **data** screens;
- Apply **import/export** of backups and **PDF generation** where implemented and unlocked;
- Show **ads** or **premium** flows when enabled in your build.

We do **not** use your client or order content to build an advertising profile **on our side**; third-party ad networks may process data under **their** policies when ads are shown.

---

## 4. Data sharing

- We **do not sell** your personal information.
- Your **atelier content** is not uploaded to **our** servers as part of the default local workflow described above.
- **App stores**, **ad networks**, and **hosts of optional remote URLs** you fetch may process data according to **their** policies when you use those services.

Any sharing of exported files, PDFs, or screenshots happens only when **you** initiate share, export, or copy.

---

## 5. Third-party services

The App may rely on third parties, including:

- **Advertising:** may collect identifiers or usage data for ad delivery and measurement.
- **Payments:** processed by the platform store.
- **Hosts of optional configuration JSON:** whoever operates the URL you fetch may see **technical request data** (such as IP address) like any web server.

Those services operate under their own terms and privacy policies.

---

## 6. Data retention and deletion

- Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
- You can use in-app options to **erase local atelier data** or **remove records** where provided.
- Uninstalling the App typically **permanently removes** local databases from the device.

## 7. Security

We apply reasonable practices in the App, including keeping atelier content **on-device** by default and minimizing unnecessary collection.

You are responsible for securing your device (PIN, password, biometrics) and keeping the OS updated.
